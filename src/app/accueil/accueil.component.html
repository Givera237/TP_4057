Operation

How it works




You post a lost or found object on the platform, giving as much information as possible about the object (the area where the object was lost or found, the description, etc.)



The matching algorithm helps us identify matches (object and owner) in the database quickly and without searching.



When the matching is successful, you will be notified of the location and address of the partner who holds your object. Then you can talk to each other to make an appointment.
